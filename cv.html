Research Interests

Broadly in Commutative Algebra and Algebraic Geometry, with strong interaction with Combinatorial/Computational methods.

  • Symbolic and rational symbolic powers; containments and asymptotic invariants (Waldschmidt constants, resurgence).
  • Interpolation problems for (fat) points in projective and multi-projective spaces.
  • Valuations, integral closure, symbolic Rees algebras; Rees valuations and symbolic polyhedra.
  • Combinatorial structures (matroids, Stanley–Reisner ideals, polyhedral techniques) connected to algebraic geometry.
