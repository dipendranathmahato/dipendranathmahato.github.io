Personal Bio

Welcome to my Personal Website.

Hi...I am Dipendranath Mahato (দীপেন্দ্রনাথ মাহাতো), aka. DIPEN.

Advisor: Prof. Tài Huy Hà .

Current position: Ph.D. Candidate, Tulane University. I am currently in the $5^{\text{th}}$ year of my Ph.D. program in the Department of Mathematics. My primary research interests lie in Commutative Algebra, Algebraic Geometry, Combinatorial Algebra. The following are the problems of my interest.

Research Interests:

  • • Symbolic and Rational Symbolic Powers of Ideals
  • • Fat-point Schemes (particularly, non-homogeneous) & Interpolation
  • • Asymptotic Invariants (Seshadri Constants, Waldschmidt Constants, Resurgence)
  • • Matroid Association to Interpolation Problem
  • • Combinatorial Hodge Theory
